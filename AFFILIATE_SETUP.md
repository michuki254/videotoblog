# LemonSqueezy Affiliate Program Setup Guide

This guide will help you enable and configure LemonSqueezy's built-in Affiliate Hub for your VideotoBlog platform.

## Step 1: Enable Affiliate Program in LemonSqueezy Dashboard

1. **Login to LemonSqueezy Dashboard**
   - Go to [app.lemonsqueezy.com](https://app.lemonsqueezy.com)
   - Sign in to your account

2. **Navigate to Affiliates Settings**
   - Go to `Settings` → `Affiliates`
   - Click "Enable Affiliate Program"

3. **Configure Affiliate Settings**
   ```
   Commission Rate: 30%
   Cookie Duration: 30 days
   Minimum Payout: $50
   Payout Schedule: Monthly
   Auto-approve affiliates: No (manual approval recommended)
   ```

4. **Set Up Affiliate Program Details**
   - **Program Name**: VideotoBlog AI Affiliate Program
   - **Description**: Earn 30% commission promoting AI-powered video-to-blog conversion
   - **Terms & Conditions**: Add your affiliate terms
   - **Commission Structure**: 
     - Basic Plan ($9.99): $3.00 per referral
     - Pro Plan ($29.99): $9.00 per referral  
     - Enterprise Plan ($99.99): $30.00 per referral
